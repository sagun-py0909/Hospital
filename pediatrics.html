Pediatrics Department

The Pediatrics Department at Lotus Hospital is dedicated to providing specialized medical care for infants, children, and adolescents. Led by Dr. Sarah Anderson, our team of pediatricians and nurses ensures compassionate and comprehensive healthcare for young patients.

Services Offered:

  • Well-child check-ups and vaccinations
  • Management of pediatric illnesses and infections
  • Developmental screenings and early interventions
  • Specialized care for chronic conditions
  • Pediatric surgery and emergency care
