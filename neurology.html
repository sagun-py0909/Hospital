Neurology Department

The Neurology Department at Lotus Hospital specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. Led by Dr. Jane Smith, our team provides advanced neurological care with a focus on patient comfort and outcomes.

Services Offered:

  • Management of stroke and cerebrovascular diseases
  • Treatment for epilepsy and seizures
  • Neuromuscular disorders
  • Headaches and migraines treatment
  • Neurorehabilitation programs
