Orthopedics Department

The Orthopedics Department at Lotus Hospital specializes in the treatment of musculoskeletal disorders and injuries. Led by Dr. Michael Johnson, our team of orthopedic surgeons and specialists provides comprehensive care using the latest surgical and non-surgical techniques.

Services Offered:

  • Joint replacement surgeries
  • Sports medicine and rehabilitation
  • Spine surgery and treatment
  • Fracture care and trauma management
  • Arthroscopic procedures
