Cardiology Department

The Cardiology Department at Lotus Hospital provides specialized care for patients with heart-related conditions and diseases. Led by Dr. John Doe, our team of cardiologists and healthcare professionals is committed to delivering comprehensive cardiac care using advanced medical technologies.

Services Offered:

  • Diagnostic tests including ECG, echocardiogram, and stress tests
  • Cardiac catheterization and angioplasty
  • Management of heart failure and arrhythmias
  • Cardiac rehabilitation programs
  • Preventive cardiology and lifestyle management
