Oncology Department

The Oncology Department at Lotus Hospital specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of cancer. Led by Dr. Robert Davis, our team of oncologists, hematologists, and oncology nurses provides personalized care and support throughout the cancer journey.

Services Offered:

  • Chemotherapy and radiation therapy
  • Surgical oncology procedures
  • Targeted therapy and immunotherapy
  • Pain management and palliative care
  • Cancer survivorship programs
